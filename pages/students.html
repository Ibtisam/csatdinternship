Eligibility Requirements

  • Completed at least 4 semesters of your degree program
  • Minimum CGPA of 2.0
  • Good academic standing with no disciplinary issues
  • Approval from academic advisor

Application Procedure

Step 1: Check Eligibility

Verify you meet all requirements before applying

Step 2: Fill Expression of Interest

Fill the Expression of Interest form for Onsite or Freelancing mode of internship

Step 3: Wait for Approval

Wait for approval from Internship Office and assigned supervisors

Step 4: Find Internship

Secure an internship placement with approved organization

Step 5: Submit Assignment Application

Download and submit the assignment application form with required documents (Internship Offer Letter)

Step 6: Get Registered

Get registered for the internship program after submission of all required documents

Step 7: Submit Design Proposal

Submit your design proposal for approval before starting the internship to allocated faculty supervisor.

Step 8: Start the Internship Journey

Learn, gain experience, and complete required documentation.

Step 9: Submit Weekly Progress

Submit weekly logs to document your progress during the internship, communicate with your faculty supervisor.

Step 10: Submit Final Report & Internship Certificate

Submit your final internship report and Internship completion Certificate.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the duration of the internship?

The internship requires a minimum of 6-8 continuous weeks of full-time work.

Can I do a virtual internship?

Yes, virtual internships are accepted and must meet the same academic standards as on-site internships.

What documents do I need to submit?

You'll need to submit Expression of Interest Form (online), Internship Offer Letter (Optional), Assignment Form (Download, Print, and Fill), Design Proposal, Weekly Logs, Internship Report.

How is the internship graded?

The internship is graded Pass/Fail based on supervisor evaluations, report quality, and weekly log submissions.
