Partner Requirements

Organization Requirements

  • Registered business or organization
  • Relevant to computer science/IT field
  • Ability to provide meaningful technical work
  • Professional work environment
  • Designated supervisor for intern

Internship Requirements

  • Minimum 6-8 week duration
  • Clear learning objectives
  • Technical project assignments
  • Regular supervision and feedback
  • Final evaluation submission

Memorandum of Understanding (MoU)

We establish formal partnerships through Memoranda of Understanding to ensure mutual benefits and clear expectations.

MoU Benefits:

  • Formal recognition as university partner
  • Priority access to student talent pool
  • Joint research and project opportunities
  • Invitation to university events and career fairs
  • Long-term collaboration framework

MoU Process:

1

Initial Discussion

Meet with internship office to discuss partnership opportunities

2

Draft Review

Review and customize MoU template

3

Finalization

Sign MoU with university authorities

4

Implementation

Begin hosting interns and collaboration
