Faculty Supervisor Role

As a Faculty Supervisor, you play a crucial role in guiding students through their internship journey and ensuring academic standards are maintained.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Review and approve internship proposals
  • Monitor student progress through weekly logs
  • Evaluate final internship reports
  • Provide academic guidance and support
  • Liaise with industry supervisors
  • Assign final grades in coordination with internship office

Evaluation Process

Initial Approval

Review and approve student internship proposals within first week

Mid-term Check

Monitor progress through weekly activity logs and provide feedback

Final Evaluation

Evaluate final report, reflective summary, and overall performance

Grade Submission

Submit final evaluation to internship office within one week of internship completion

Industry Supervisor Role

As an Industry Supervisor, you provide real-world guidance and ensure students gain valuable professional experience.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Provide meaningful technical work assignments
  • Introduce student to organizational culture and practices
  • Monitor daily performance and provide regular feedback
  • Complete mid-term and final evaluations
  • Ensure a safe and professional work environment
  • Communicate with faculty supervisor as needed

Evaluation Criteria

Technical Skills

Application of technical knowledge, problem-solving abilities

Professionalism

Punctuality, work ethic, professional conduct

Communication

Verbal and written communication skills

Teamwork

Collaboration and interpersonal skills
