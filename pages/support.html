Frequently Asked Questions

Student FAQs

When should I start looking for an internship?

We recommend starting your search at least 2-3 months before your intended start date. This gives you enough time to find suitable opportunities and complete the approval process.

Can I do an internship with a company not on the approved list?

Yes, you can arrange your own internship. However, the organization and proposed work must be approved by the internship office before you begin.

Partner FAQs

What are the benefits of becoming a partner organization?

Partner organizations get access to talented students, opportunity to shape future professionals, potential recruitment pipeline, and academic collaboration opportunities.

How many interns can we host?

There's no strict limit, but we recommend starting with 1-2 interns to ensure proper supervision and meaningful experiences.

General FAQs

What is the duration of the internship?

The internship requires a minimum of 6-8 continuous weeks of full-time equivalent work.

Are virtual internships accepted?

Yes, virtual internships are accepted and must meet the same academic standards as on-site internships.
